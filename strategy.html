TCJ Smart Volume Concept

A structured crypto futures framework focused on leverage quality, execution discipline, and continuation validity.

Core Pipeline

  • SCC/TCC context first
  • 15m AEV anchor
  • VDI stack and slope confirmation
  • Trendline + volume + liquidity validation

Execution Rules

  • No Tactical MC = no trade

  • Prioritize clean continuation over noisy momentum
